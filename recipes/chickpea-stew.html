Chickpea Stew

Description

This amazing chickpea stew will leave you coming back for more! It's full of protein and vegan too, so you don't need to feel guilty about having seconds.

Ingredients

  • Two cans of chickpeas
  • One can of chopped tomatoes
  • One onion, chopped
  • One carrot, chopped
  • Three cloves of garlic, minced
  • One cup of red lentils
  • One tablespoon of cumin
  • One tablespoon of cinnamon
  • One tablespoon of tumeric
  • Half tablespoon of salt
  • Two cups of vegetable broth

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a slow cooker
  2. Cook for 4 to 5 hours on a medium heat
  3. If stew is too liquidy remove lid for last hour
  4. Serve with couscous and warm pita bread
